Prevention Tips

Here are some prevention tips for common viral diseases:

  • Influenza: Get vaccinated annually and practice good hygiene.
  • Dengue: Use mosquito repellents and avoid stagnant water.
  • Chickenpox: Maintain good hygiene, keep infected individuals isolated, and consider vaccination for prevention.
